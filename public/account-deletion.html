PicFlick Account Deletion

Last updated: May 4, 2026

This page explains how PicFlick users can delete their account and data.

Delete your account in the app (recommended)

  1. Open PicFlick.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion in the final confirmation step.
Can’t access the app?

Email siv19791974@gmail.com from the email address linked to your PicFlick account and request account deletion.

What data is deleted

  • Account profile and login association
  • Uploaded photos and related app content
  • Comments and reactions
  • Friend/follow relationships
  • Notifications associated with your account

What data may be retained temporarily

Limited technical logs, legal compliance records, and backup data may be retained for up to 30 days for security, fraud prevention, and legal obligations.
