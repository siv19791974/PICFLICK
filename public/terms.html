6. Mythic Monthly Draw Terms

The Mythic Monthly Draw is a free loyalty programme held on the 1st of every month. No purchase is required to participate.

6.1 Eligibility

Your active upload streak must meet the monthly threshold to enter. Thresholds progress each month the draw runs:

  • Month 1: 10 days
  • Month 2: 30 days
  • Month 3: 50 days
  • Month 4: 70 days
  • Month 5 onwards: 100 days

If no users qualify in a given month, the threshold does not increase for the following month.

6.2 Winners & Prizes

Three winners are selected each month via a streak-weighted lottery. Every 10 days of your streak earns one entry ticket.

  • 1st place: 3 months PRO + Gold Crown + Profile Banner
  • 2nd place: 1 month PRO + Silver Crown + Profile Banner
  • 3rd place: 2 weeks PRO + Bronze Crown + Profile Banner

If a winner is already on PRO, they receive ULTRA instead. If a winner is already on ULTRA, their ULTRA tier is extended and they receive a permanent Mythic Champion badge.

6.3 All Entrants

Every user who enters the draw but does not win receives a +30% daily upload boost for 30 days and progresses their Mythic tier badge (Bronze, Silver, Gold, or Diamond) based on consecutive months entered.

6.4 Repeat Protection

A user who wins any placed prize may not win again for 3 months. They remain eligible for all-entrant rewards during this period.
