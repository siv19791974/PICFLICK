PicFlick Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

1. Introduction

PicFlick ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application PicFlick (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address, profile photo, and display name.
  • Phone Number: With your permission, we may access your phone number for authentication and friend discovery purposes.
  • Contacts: With your permission, we access your contacts to help you find friends who are already using PicFlick.

2.2 User Content

  • Photos and Media: Photos you upload, share, or take through the App.
  • Captions and Comments: Text content you add to your photos.
  • Reactions: Your reactions (likes, hearts, etc.) on other users' content.

2.3 Usage Information

  • App usage statistics and analytics
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Crash logs and error reports

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • To enable photo sharing with your friends
  • To facilitate chat and messaging between users
  • To help you find and connect with friends
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To send you important notifications about your account

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Photos and content you share are visible to your friends on the platform based on your privacy settings.
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for hosting, storage, and authentication. These providers have their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Privacy Settings

PicFlick is built with privacy-by-default principles:

  • You control who can see your photos (friends only or public)
  • You can block users at any time
  • You can delete your photos and account
  • Friends must accept your friend request before seeing your content

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage
  • Access to your data is restricted and logged

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Update your profile information
  • Control your privacy settings
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

8. Permissions We Use

  • Camera: To take photos within the App
  • Storage: To save and upload photos
  • Contacts: To help you find friends (optional)
  • Phone State: For phone number verification (optional)
  • Internet: To connect to our services
  • Notifications: To send you activity alerts

9. Children's Privacy

PicFlick is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App.

11. Account Deletion Request

PicFlick users can request account and data deletion directly in the app:

  1. Open PicFlick
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by emailing siv19791974@gmail.com from the email linked to your PicFlick account.

What is deleted: account profile, uploaded photos, comments, reactions, friend connections, notifications, and app content associated with your account.

What may be retained temporarily: limited technical logs, legal compliance records, and backup data for up to 30 days for fraud prevention, security, and legal obligations.
