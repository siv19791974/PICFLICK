# PicFlick Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 3, 2026

## Introduction

PicFlick ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use the PicFlick mobile application and related services.

By using PicFlick, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we collect:
- Your Google account email address
- Your display name (as provided by Google or set within PicFlick)
- Your profile photo URL
- A unique user identifier (UID)

You may also optionally provide:
- A bio/description
- A custom profile photo

### 2. Photos and Content
- Photos and images you upload through the app
- Captions and descriptions you add to photos
- Comments you post on photos
- Reactions (emoji responses) you leave on photos
- Tagged friends in photos

### 3. Social Interaction Data
- Friend connections and follow relationships
- Friend requests sent and received
- Group/album memberships
- Chat messages and conversations
- Blocked and muted user lists

### 4. Device and Usage Information
- Device type and operating system version
- App usage analytics (via Firebase Analytics)
- Crash reporting data (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- Push notification tokens (for delivering notifications)

### 5. Purchase Information
- Subscription tier and status (Free, Standard, Plus, Pro, Ultra)
- Purchase history (managed via Google Play Billing)
- Storage usage metrics

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide the core service** — displaying your photos in your friends' feeds, enabling chat, and managing social connections
2. **Send notifications** — alerting you to friend requests, messages, reactions, and comments via push notifications
3. **Ensure safety** — enabling content reporting, blocking, and moderation features
4. **Improve the app** — analyzing crashes and usage patterns to fix bugs and enhance features
5. **Manage subscriptions** — processing in-app purchases and subscription renewals through Google Play
6. **Enforce limits** — tracking storage usage and daily upload quotas based on your subscription tier

---

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored using **Google Firebase** services (Firestore, Cloud Storage, and Cloud Functions)
- Photos are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage with access controlled by Firebase Security Rules
- Profile and social data is stored in Firebase Firestore with authenticated access controls
- We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data

---

## Data Sharing

### With Other Users
- Your **public profile** (display name, profile photo, bio) is visible to other PicFlick users
- Photos you set to **"Public"** are visible to all PicFlick users
- Photos you set to **"Friends Only"** are visible only to users you follow who also follow you back (mutual friends)
- Your **chat messages** are visible only to participants in that conversation

### With Service Providers
We use the following third-party services that may process your data:
- **Google Firebase** — data storage, authentication, analytics, and crash reporting
- **Google Play Services** — in-app purchase processing and billing
- **Google Cloud** — image processing and thumbnail generation

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

---

## User Rights and Controls

You have the following rights and controls:

- **Privacy Settings** — Choose "Friends Only" or "Public" for each photo you upload
- **Delete Photos** — Permanently delete any photo you have uploaded
- **Block Users** — Prevent specific users from seeing your content or interacting with you
- **Mute Users** — Temporarily hide a user's content from your feed
- **Delete Account** — Permanently remove all your data from PicFlick via Settings > Delete Account
- **Manage Notifications** — Control which notification types you receive

---

## Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- **Deleted photos:** Removed immediately from your profile and friends' feeds
- **Deleted accounts:** All personal data, photos, and social connections are permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Anonymized analytics:** Crash and usage data may be retained in anonymized form for app improvement

---

## Children's Privacy

PicFlick is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete the information.

---

## International Data Transfers

Your data is stored and processed on servers located in the United States (via Google Firebase). By using PicFlick, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.
