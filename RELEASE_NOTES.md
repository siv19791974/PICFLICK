## What's New in Version 1.0

### Core Features
- **Private Photo Sharing** - Share moments with friends in a secure, private space
- **Real-time Chat** - Message friends directly within the app
- **Photo Filters** - Apply beautiful filters before uploading
- **Friend Discovery** - Find and connect with friends easily
- **Reaction System** - Like and react to photos with emojis

### Photo & Camera
- High quality photo uploads (100% JPEG)
- Portrait mode locked for consistent experience
- Smooth pinch-to-zoom in photo viewer
- Filter thumbnails in 12 languages

### Profile & Social
- Customizable profile with photo and bio
- Friend following system
- Photo streaks to encourage daily sharing
- Tiered subscription plans (Free, Standard, Plus, Pro, Ultra)

### Privacy & Security
- Friends-only sharing by default
- Block/unblock users
- No ads or algorithms
- Your data stays private

### International Support
- **12 Languages Supported:**
  - English (UK)
  - Arabic (العربية)
  - Spanish (Español)
  - French (Français)
  - German (Deutsch)
  - Chinese (中文)
  - Japanese (日本語)
  - Korean (한국어)
  - Portuguese (Português)
  - Albanian (Shqip)
  - Hindi (हिन्दी)
  - Greek (Ελληνικά)
